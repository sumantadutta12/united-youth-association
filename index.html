About Us

A Youth-Led Community Association Working With Local Families

United Youth Association began two months ago with a small group of volunteers and a shared promise to serve with honesty. We focus on immediate needs first, then build long-term support through education, health awareness, and community care.

  • Started with local youth volunteers
  • Focused on transparent community support
  • Food and essentials for families in need
  • Education support for children
  • Health and awareness activities
  • Elderly care and doorstep help
