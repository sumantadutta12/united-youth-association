Floodlight Football Cup

United Youth Association invites local teams to the Gill Cup, a 16-team floodlight mini-bar football tournament in memory of Jaswant Singh Gill.

Date 18 July 2026, Saturday, 5:00 PM
Venue Union Board Ground, New Egara, Raniganj
Format Ultimate 16-Team Mini-Bar Tournament
Prize 1st Prize Rs. 7000, Runner Up Rs. 5000 + Trophy

Team Entry

Entry fee is Rs. 1100 with Rs. 500 advance. Teams should confirm participation early.

Match Rules

7-a-side plus 2 extra players. League matches are 10 minutes and the final is 20 minutes.

Contact Team

Sourav, Arpan, Souvick, and Bubai are coordinating registration and tournament support.
